twinlist :

rethinking medication reconciliation

We present a new take on two-list medication reconciliation: a novel interface built on spatial grouping and staged animation.

The examples here are of hospital discharge scenarios.

Desktop controls :

  • click on a drug to decide :
    • left-click once to keep
    • right-click once to reject
    • click repeatedly to undo

Touch-device controls :

  • tap on a drug to highlight and see additional information
    • left-swipe once to keep
    • right-swipe once to reject
    • swipe repeatedly to undo

Common controls :

  • you can also use "keep rest" and "reject rest" to decide on multiple items in a column
  • click sign off when finished
  • (additional options also available by clicking "show options")
